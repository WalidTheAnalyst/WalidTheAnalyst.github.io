Who am I?

Hey! I'm Walid: a young and curious Moroccan data analyst currently enrolled at the University of Toronto. I've done some cool projects that you can check out!

I am a fourth-year Economics and Statistics student with a keen passion for quantitative data analysis. My interest in this field began in September 2019, when I enrolled in my first ever statistics course: STA130 - An Introduction to Statistical Reasoning and Data Science. This is where I discovered the fundamentals of linear regression, learned about null vs. alternative hypotheses, and got acquainted with the programming language R. Learning R felt like picking up a new language, much like learning the basics of English verbs.

In my spare time, I engage in activities that improve various aspects of my lifestyle. Chess improves my problem-solving skills, and tennis and football (soccer) keep me in shape without the boredom of a treadmill. Lastly, strumming the guitar provides me with a creative outlet for self-expression.

If you are looking for a French and English-speaking data analyst who is motivated and curious to learn, do not hesitate to drop an email at: walid.el.roudani@mail.utoronto.ca !
